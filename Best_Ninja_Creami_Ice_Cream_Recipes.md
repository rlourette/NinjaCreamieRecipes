## Easy Ninja Creami Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe

### Description
This easy Ninja Creami vanilla ice cream is rich and decadent, but without the effort of traditional ice cream recipes!

### Ingredients
- 1 TBSP (1/2 ounce) cream cheese
- 1/3 cup granulated sugar
- 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup whole milk
- 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream

### Instructions
1. In a mixing bowl, combine the cream cheese, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract. Whisk until smooth.
2. Gradually add the whole milk and heavy whipping cream, whisking until the sugar is completely dissolved and the mixture is well combined.
3. Pour the mixture into your Ninja Creami pint container.
4. Place the lid on the pint container and freeze for 24 hours.
5. After freezing, remove the pint from the freezer and place it into the Ninja Creami machine.
6. Select the "Ice Cream" setting and let the machine process the mixture.
7. Once processed, you can enjoy your vanilla ice cream immediately or use the "Re-spin" function if you prefer a different texture.
8. Serve and enjoy your homemade vanilla ice cream!

### Notes
- You can substitute 2% milk for whole milk if desired.
- For a richer flavor, you can adjust the sugar and vanilla extract to taste.
- If your ice cream has a greasy film or aftertaste, ensure all utensils and the Ninja Creami container are clean and free from residues.

### Additional Tips
- Experiment with mix-ins like chocolate chips, fruit, or nuts after the initial processing for added texture and flavor.
- Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the freezer for up to one week.

## Ninja Creami Rocky Road Ice Cream

### Description
Rocky Road ice cream starts off with a creamy chocolate base. Then you add in marshmallows and almonds, which bring flavor and also fun variations in texture.

### Ingredients
- 1 cup chocolate milk
- 1 packet sugar-free chocolate pudding mix
- 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
- 1/2 cup mini marshmallows
- 1/2 cup chopped almonds
- 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

### Instructions
1. In your pint container, combine chocolate milk, pudding mix, and cocoa powder. Whisk to combine.
2. Add mini marshmallows and chopped almonds to the mixture. If using, add chocolate chips as well.
3. Cover the pint and freeze for 24 hours.
4. Use your Ninja Creami machine on the "Ice Cream" setting.
5. Once processed, enjoy your Rocky Road ice cream immediately or use the "Re-spin" function if you prefer a different texture.

### Notes
- For a dairy-free treat, you can use nondairy milk (almond milk, cashew milk, oat milk, etc.) instead of dairy milk.
- You can also use canned coconut cream instead of heavy cream.

## Ninja Creami Root Beer Float Ice Cream

### Description
A favorite fountain treat, frozen! Try it today with the Ninja CREAMi.

### Ingredients
- 1 TBSP cream cheese
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1 tsp root beer extract
- 1 cup whole milk
- 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream

### Instructions
1. In a large microwave-safe bowl, add the cream cheese and microwave for 10 seconds.
2. Add the sugar and root beer extract, and with a whisk or rubber spatula, combine until the mixture looks like frosting, about 60 seconds.
3. Slowly mix in the whole milk and heavy whipping cream until fully combined and the sugar is dissolved.
4. Pour the mixture into your Ninja Creami pint container.
5. Place the lid on the pint container and freeze for 24 hours.
6. After freezing, remove the pint from the freezer and place it into the Ninja Creami machine.
7. Select the "Ice Cream" setting and let the machine process the mixture.
8. Once processed, enjoy your Root Beer Float ice cream immediately or use the "Re-spin" function if you prefer a different texture.
